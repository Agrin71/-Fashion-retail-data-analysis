## Example Prediction

To see how the model works in practice, a sample customer was evaluated.

The model estimated that this order has around a **40% probability of being returned**, indicating a **moderate level of risk**.

In a real-world setting, this type of prediction can help businesses take simple actions to reduce returns, such as improving product descriptions or assisting customers in making more accurate choices.
